Who We Are

Roland Ochoa heads the critical case unit at an award winning law firm in Florida. Over the last twelve years, he has worked with the team's unique Forensic Discovery Methodology which allows the firm to reverse-engineer the scene of an accident, injury, or product malfunction. Along with engineers, doctors, and scientists, his team traces the root causes of a personal tragedy back to its earliest and truest origin: often all the way back to the manufacturing facility or corporate boardroom.
